UniFi-Cloudflare

Dynamic DNS

Cloudflare DDNS for UniFi OS

Have a UniFi Dream Machine Pro (UDM-Pro)?
Want to update your Cloudflare DNS records when your public IP address changes?
Unfortunately, UniFi does not come pre-configured to support Cloudflare for DDNS providers.
...but there's a solution!

Configuring UniFi OS
  1. Log on to your UniFi OS Controller
  2. Navigate to Settings > Internet > WAN and scroll down to Dynamic DNS
  3. Click Create New Dynamic DNS and enter the following information:
    • Service: dyndns
    • Hostname: your subdomain/hostname record
    • Username: your domain name
    • Password: your Cloudflare API Token
    • Server: unificloudflareddns.com/update?ip=%i&hostname=

Important Note!
On UniFi devices older than the UDM, the Server value should be configured as:
unificloudflareddns.com
